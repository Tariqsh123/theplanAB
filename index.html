    Acceleration
    Program

    The Acceleration Program at Plan AB is for startups that are looking to scale with a special focus on Fintech, mCommerce, Food & Agri Tech, EdTech, Cleantech, HealthTech, and other key verticals. The program is cohort-based and we want to ensure that we have the most diverse set of startups in every cycle.
    The Program is structured to ensure our founders derive maximum benefit. The Program will take a deep dive into each accelerated startup that is part of the cohort. Generally, we will assist each startup with advice, links to relevant mentors, and a connection to networks and resources. The Program will start with an orientation session and then will have one-on-one sessions with The Plan AB team to discuss areas of assistance. The Program will have Founder Circles which is an opportunity for the founders to get acquainted with each other and other founders in the ecosystem. This is particularly important as it allows like-minded individuals to connect with others on the same journey as them. The cohort will conclude with a Demo Day – a day that focuses the attention of the community on the startups through the hosting of a series of pitches for investors, media, and the wider community.
    We are looking for founders who want to grow their business and make a difference in the startup ecosystem  We look forward to growing with you!

    Women's Leadership
    Fellows Program

    We firmly believe that the future is female  which is why we want to play our role in encouraging women leaders! We designed the WLFP for women professionals & entrepreneurs who want to accelerate their careers and become leaders in their respective fields. With time the WLFP aims to increase the number of women leaders in the economy.

    We have divided the Program into weekly training and leadership sessions. The focus of these sessions will be on goal development, resilience building, personality and leadership development, and confidence-building. The sessions will be conducted by industry experts who have the expertise & experience in their relevant fields to lead the discussion. The program includes one on one sessions with the Plan AB team to identify the fellows’ strengths and weaknesses. WFLP also aims to develop a high-powered network for these women, with senior business leaders and strong linkages with a community of like-minded peers. We also plan to hold regular fellows meetings called circles during the program, where women can connect deeply & engage with each other and learn from the experiences of the other fellows who are on the journey as them.
