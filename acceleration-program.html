    The Plan AB Acceleration Program is designed for founders ready to scale their startups with flexibility and expert support. Whether you're refining your business model or preparing to raise funds, our program offers the structure and guidance needed to grow strategically.

    Founders gain access to one-on-one mentorship from industry professionals, along with a powerful network that includes investors and the Google for Startups Mentor Network. You'll receive hands-on assistance with financial planning, pitching, and fundraising strategy—everything you need to accelerate your journey.

    What makes this program truly special is our Founders Circles—a space where entrepreneurs connect, collaborate, and support each other. It’s more than just mentorship; it's about building a strong community with others who are on the same path.
